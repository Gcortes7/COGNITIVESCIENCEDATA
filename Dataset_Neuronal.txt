4. Relevant Information:
    The paper describes the importance and use of neuroimaging using advanced techniques 
    to explore the molecular processes and cognitive activities of the brain. 
    Magnetoencephalography (MEG) and electroencephalography (EEG) are used to map
    brain activity. The following are the activities to be carried out to achieve 
    reading EEG biosignals with wireless connectivity tool used to 
    Explore the human brain and provide access to brain data in a simple way 
    and generate a brain mapping using a non-invasive technological device 
    (EMOTIV X or Insight).
